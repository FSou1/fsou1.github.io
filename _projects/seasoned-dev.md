As part of my project, SeasonedDev, I created a YouTube and Telegram channels where I shared my knowledge with developers.

On my YouTube channel, I recorded and uploaded videos on topics related to React, [Node.js](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jma5FVjjxMk), and [Deno](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BS0tq9dtr7Y), as well as coding projects like [how to parse Google Maps](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5xOD4-M2jSw). I also shared tips and tricks that could help developers improve their skills and become more effective programmers.

In addition to my YouTube channel, I also created a Telegram channel where I posted notes about programming. These notes covered a wide range of topics. I helped my audience become better programmers and stay updated with the latest trends and technologies by sharing insights and advice.

Through SeasonedDev, I aimed to create a resource for developers who are looking to improve their skills and stay up-to-date with the latest developments in the programming world. I am passionate about sharing my expertise and insights with the developer community, and I hope that my content has made a significant contribution to the field.
