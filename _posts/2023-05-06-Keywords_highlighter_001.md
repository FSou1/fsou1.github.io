I'm excited to announce that the first version of my new Chrome extension, [Keywords Highlighter](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/keywords-highlighter/lmaonnpepldfnbkbmhkkficpmjdlojnn), is now available for download on the Chrome Web Store! You can also find the code on the project's GitHub repository.

Keywords Highlighter is a fast and efficient way to find words on web pages. With just a few clicks, users can highlight specific keywords to quickly locate relevant information. This can significantly increase the speed of surfing the internet and make browsing much more efficient.

One of the best things about Keywords Highlighter is how fast it works. The extension is designed to be lightweight and operate seamlessly in the background while you browse. And with a variety of customization options, you can tailor the extension to fit your specific needs and preferences.

So whether you're looking for a faster way to find information online or just want to streamline your browsing experience, I believe this is a great tool to help you do it.

p.s. Any feedback, bug reports, and feature requests are always welcome! Feel free to submit them on the project's [GitHub repository](https://github.com/FSou1/Keywords-Highlighter).
