During my third year studying Computer Science at university, I began my career as a full stack software developer. It was my first job, and I joined a small team of skilled software and QA engineers. The Ministry of Transport utilized our project for the purpose of managing the collection, issuance, and verification of airport radio navigational certificates.

Through my work on this project, I gained extensive knowledge in various areas such as FrontEnd development with JavaScript, backend development with C# and ASP.NET, as well as designing Microsoft SQL server database, developing stored procedures, and optimizing query performance.

## My contribution

- Developed an automated information system for accounting, security analysis and certification airport's radio navigational devices in the national registry.
